About Game



Valorant is an online multiplayer computer game, produced by Riot Games. It is a first-person shooter game, consisting of two teams of five, where one team attacks and the other defends. Players control characters known as agents, who all have different abilities to use during gameplay. The games matchmaking system automatically groups players of similar skill together.
